IT Asset Disposition

Coordinate labor and packaging

ServerMonkey will coordinate on-site labor to manage the entire decommissioning process. Our packaging and transportation practices will adhere to your data security requirements and protect the remarket potential of assets during transportation.

Research marketability

Discover the hidden value in your retired assets through ServerMonkey's marketability assessments. Our asset recovery team will assess market conditions, compare full-unit versus component value, and maximize your ROI potential.

Wipe HDD & SDD data

ServerMonkey's different data wiping options, with algorithms for 1-35 passes, can meet whatever level of data security you require, permanently clearing information from your drives to Department of Defense (DoD) and National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) standards.

Uncable, unrack, and palletize hardware

ServerMonkey's experienced technicians will remove assets and get them ready for shipment to the ServerMonkey processing center. All hardware will be carefully de-installed and palletized.

Refurbish or recycle, as appropriate

ServerMonkey's refurbishment capabilities can often increase an asset's marketability. If remarketing is not an option, or if security protocols require destruction, our ISO certified information destruction and recycling services provide environmentally compliant and fully documented asset disposal.

Maintain traceability

ServerMonkey's Certificates of Destruction and Certificates of Data Erasure document each shredding and wiping job we carry out, verifying that wiped data has been erased entirely and that shredded drives are completely destroyed
